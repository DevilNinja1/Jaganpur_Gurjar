About Covirest

COVIREST is a COVID19 RESPONSE PROGRAM initiated by the MJ Trust. It is a volunteer run program set up by medicos, technologists and professionals from India and abroad to aid the fight against COVID19.

85% COVID19 patients with LOW RISK infections, can be treated at home itself. Our aim is to ensure more people can avail quality and timely access to medical and protocol driven COVID19 treatments at home, thus ensuring the remaining 15% CRITICIAL infections can have uninterrupted and equitable access to CRITICAL & INTENSIVE healthcare.

Treatment

Our emphasis is on scientific and medical protocol driven
treatment and interventions:
                    
1 Early Detection of COVID19 in patients
2 Constant Monitoring of Key Indicators
3 Timely intervention of different classes of medication, dependent on 
patient case.
4 Adoption of globally recognized medical and clinical procedures and
 treatment protocol, versus non scientific approach currently prevalent
 in India.
